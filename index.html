Comfort Foods is like a family. Lets make it work for all of us!

Here are the guidelines when coming to work at the facility:

  • Make sure if you are bringing your car, park in an actual parking spot. Not on the lawn. Facility parking lot is still under construction for ten years.
  • Please get a parking pass or show a parking pass to the security guard. Do not run past the guard, you'll be fired on the spot.
  • Make sure you are signed in at every shift or signed out. You will not be paid if you forget!
  • Hair nets and beard nets must be worn as soon as you enter the kitchen. If you are bald, speak to management.
  • ALL kitchen tasks must be finished before you are dismissed. You will be penalized if there is work left over!
  • If the dishwasher is broken, you must wash the utensils by hand.
  • Do not talk to other staff members while doing your task. It distracts others from their work. BE RESPECTFUL!
  • Take breaks at proper times, and do not eat on the units while serving the residents.

Staffing policy

  • Only the manager can approve time off. If you already booked a vacation, it can be cancelled at the last minute!
  • Shifts will be assigned two days in advance. You must work them even if it's in a 24hr timeframe.
  • If you call out sick, you must require a doctor’s note and vital chart. If you cannot provide both, you’ll be penalized or fired!
  • When calling off work, you must always swap shifts! If you do not get a replacement, you will be penalized!
  • If there’s a death in your family, you must ask for time off. There is no guarantee you’ll get any time off. We are understaffed.
  • Remember: WE ARE YOUR ONLY FAMILY! 😄

Shift Guidelines and Procedures

Morning / Afternoon Shift – 7:00 am – 2:30 pm

7:00 am – Arrive in the kitchen and check in with the manager to sign in. After signing in, start prepping for breakfast. Peel 100 boiled eggs for residents and cut cheeses. This task must be done in 30 minutes or less.

7:30 am – Start packing and preparing breakfast in the kitchen unit. Brew coffee and tea. YOU ONLY HAVE 30 MINUTES! Make sure items that have expired have been thrown out. If the employee did not do their task the evening before, please report them to management. See Snitches get Riches for more information.

8:00 am – You must start serving the residents right away. Make sure PSWs and other aides assist regardless even if they refuse. Start with morning beverages. The residents must have fluids.

9:30 am – Employees must stop serving residents. Nursing staff must figure out what to feed residents after this time. The kitchen must be cleaned and wiped down for lunch service. Once finished, leave out the nourishment cart and head down to the main kitchen to sort silverware.

10:00 am – Once you are in the main kitchen, you must sort silverware, clean carts, and set up lunch menu items. DO NOT PREP FOOD YET, ONLY AFTER 45 MIN BREAK! After this kitchen task is finished, head for break.

10:30 am - 11:15 am – BREAK TIME!
